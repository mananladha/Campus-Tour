About VIT Bhopal.

Pioneering Excellence in Education Since 2017

Established in 2017, VIT Bhopal is spread over a huge campus of 250 acres located at Indore Highway, Bhopal. The campus has a unique integrated 'Lecture and Laboratory' learning platform which is the blackboard-less classroom.

The university uses Collaborative and Active Learning Through Technology (CALTECH) and a Fully Flexible Credit System (FFCS) to facilitate the learning of students.
